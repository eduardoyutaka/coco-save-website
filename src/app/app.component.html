7 Tips for Coco Save Beginners


Posted on 30/04/2018

"Keep your chick alive as long as possible avoiding geometric objects like circles, squares and sticks.
Become Coco Save Champion!"

Carlo D'Avino

Introduction

The dark background and warm color palette on top of the haptics feedback makes the game extremely engaging and, in a certain wat, satisfatory.
Built on top of simple graphics and collision mechanics, this app make every developer say "I could have made this app".

Naming Convention

Coco: The bird.

Orb: The cursor.

Block: The rectangular falling object.

Stick: The long falling object.

Marble The circular falling object.

Tip #1

Coco always behave in the same way.

  1. It starts at the center.
  2. Moves to the left.
  3. Back the the center.
  4. Moves to the right.
  5. Back to the center.
  6. Repeat...

Tip #2

You can never lost on the first wave of objects by standing still.

Tip #3

Objects always fall from left to right except for the horizontal sticks, which stack at the center.

Tip #4

Each wave of objects spawns every 2 seconds.

Tip #5

Marbles have a larger hitbox than boxes. At the central position, Coco can get hit by the second marble from the left, which doesn't happen with the boxes.

Tip #6

You can move the orb up and down.
If you haven't passed the 30" for the first time yet, try to keep the orb as close to Coco as possible.

Tip #7

Try to only block the necessary objects.
Most of the times, you only need to hit one or two objects. This makes your moves more efficient.
